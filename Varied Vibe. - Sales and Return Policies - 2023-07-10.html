Sales and Return Policies

Our Return Policy

Your business is important to us! We want you to be more than happy and satisfied with your order and your product.

  • If you’re not satisfied with your order, you can return or exchange any item(s) within 30 days of its purchase date. Items that have been washed, worn, or used may not be returned.
  • If for any reason you receive a defective or damaged product, Varied Vibe. will refund the total order purchase amount, including return shipping cost.
  • Please contact customer service at contact@variedvibe.com for all returns.
  • Once approved for a return, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will be automatically applied to your credit card or original method of payment. Please allow for a period of up to 14 business days for us to process the refund.
